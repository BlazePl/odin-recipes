Pizza

Not only fast, but tasty and healthy!

Four steps method

  1. Step

    2. Preheat oven to 220°C. Grease and lightly flour a large baking tray.

  2. Step

    3. Prepare the pizza dough using the pizza base kit and water following packet directions. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth. Use a rolling pin to roll out to a 25cm disc. Place on the prepared tray. Set aside in a warm place for 10 mins.

  3. Step

    4. Spread the pizza base with pizza sauce. Top with salami.

  4. Step

    5. Bake for 20 mins or until cheese melts and base is golden.

Ingredients

  • 285g pkt Lion Pizza Base Kit
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) warm water
  • 2 tbsp Leggo’s Pizza Sauce Garlic, Onion & Herbs
  • 85g salami slices

And that's it, have a great meal!
